Nominal Use Cases

  1. Player fulfills mission
  2. Player piece together current element tokens
  3. Player accomplish objective by building up the 3D compound
  4. Player lands on a chance square

Off-Nominal Use Cases

  1. One player leaves the game half-way
  2. Player trades objective token with another player after fulfilling mission

Nominal Use Cases

1. Player fulfills mission (players discuss and agree on the validity)

Initial Condition

- Player lands on the mission square.

- Player has the corresponding element card in his hand.


Player Other Players Element Cards Objective Tokens
Player checks the cards in his hand and select one card
The element card will be shown to other players
Other players discuss about whether the shown card is valid or not
One objective token will be awarded to the player if the card is valid
A new element card will be given to player

Ending Condition

- Player receives the token.

- Total number of cards in playerâ€™s hand remains constant.

- Players employ deeper thinking about chemistry concepts through their discussions.



2. Player pieces together current objective tokens collected

Initial Condition

- Player has fulfilled mission square.


Player Objective Cards Objective Tokens
Objective card shows the player elements left required to complete mission
Player identifies a token to to take as reward
Token is awarded to the player
Player pieces newly obtained token to the previously obtained ones

Ending Condition

- Player fulfils a sub-part of his overall objective.

- Player gains hands-on experience of creating compound structure.

- Player gets an understanding of the 3D layout of chemical compounds.


3. Player accomplish objective by building up the 3D compound

Initial Condition

- Player has gathered all the pieces of token that are required for the 3D compound.


Player Other Players Objective Card Objective Tokens
All the tokens will be combined into a compound
Objective card will be used as instruction
Player declares that the objective has been accomplished
Other players will compare the Objective Card and the combined tokens
Player will declare the win if the objective is fulfilled correctly

Ending Condition

- The game ends.

- Player gains spatial understanding of compound structures.



4. Player lands on a chance square (eg. that asks to discard a toxic element)

Initial Condition

- Player is on chance square.


Player Chance Card Element Card
Chance card is given to player
Player reads the contents of the chance card
Player decides which element card to use to fulfil chance card requirement
Replenishment element card given to player

Ending Condition

- Player ends turn.

- Player gains understanding of applications of the knowledge in real life.



Off-Nominal Use Cases

1. One player leaves the game half-way

Initial Condition

- The game is ongoing.


Player Element Cards Objective Tokens
Player decides to leave the game
Player announces the retirement from the game
Element Cards are discarded
Tokens collected are dismantled and returned back to the pile

Ending Condition

- Players continue to play with one less player.



2. Player trades objective token with another player after fulfilling mission

Initial Condition

- Player able to fulfill the mission but does not need any of the tokens available.


Player Objective Tokens Other Player
Player declares that he does not need any of the tokens available
Player declares a token that he would like to have
Other player checks to see if he has the token the player mentioned
Other player decides if he would like to trade for a token available on the mission square
Tokens are swapped

Ending Condition

- Player obtains token of choice.
