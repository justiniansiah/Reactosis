Design Rules

Sub-teams Responsibilities
Logic Designers
  • Come up with rules that govern the overall game
  • Design content that will be used throughout the game (mission squares, element cards, tokens, chance cards and objective cards)
  • Ensure that there is consistency in gameplay (e.g. If the objective cards requires the player to obtain 5 carbon atoms, there must be at least 5 carbon tokens available for collection)
Artifact Designers
  • Design and fabrication of game pieces, chance cards, element cards and tokens
Board Designers
  • Design and fabrication of game board and overall packaging.

Early Design Decisions

  1. The tokens, content of the mission squares, element cards and objective cards have to be decided
    • The Logic Designers would need to come up with this before the Artifact and Board Designers can design the graphics and fabricate them.
  2. The design of the cardback of the element cards and chance cards have to be decided
    • The Artifact Designers would need to confirm the designs as they will be used on the game board by the Board Designers
