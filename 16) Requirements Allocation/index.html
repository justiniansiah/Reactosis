Requirements Allocation

  1. Board designer shall create a board that is compact.

  2. Board designer shall create a board that is waterproof.

  3. Board designer shall create a board that weighs more than 0.5kg.

  4. Artifact designer shall design the pieces such that they are not a choking hazard.

  5. Artifact designer shall ensure that there are extra board pieces (player pieces and tokens).

  6. Artifact designer shall ensure all the game pieces have no sharp edges.

  7. Artifact designer material cost shall not exceed SGD 150.

  8. Board designer material cost shall not exceed SGD 50.

  9. Artifact designer shall produce board pieces that are recyclable.

  10. Artifact designer shall ensure that the tokens shall be sufficient for players to form the objective molecule, with a total quantity of at least 60 tokens.

  11. Artifact designer shall ensure that the token connectors are sufficient for players to piece the tokens together, with a total quantity of at least 100 connectors.

  12. Logic designer shall come up with content that ensures that a single round of the game takes at most 1 hour to complete, with 90% confidence.

  13. Logic designer and board designer shall ensure that there are at least 30 squares on the board.
