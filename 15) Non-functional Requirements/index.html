Non-functional Requirements

  1. The board game packaging shall be compact, with at most a volume of 3200cm3.

  2. The board game shall be waterproof.

  3. The board game shall not be blown away by the wind, weighing at least 0.5kg.

  4. The board game pieces shall not be a choking hazard, with at least a surface area of 3.14cm2.

  5. The board pieces shall be replaceable.

  6. The board pieces shall not contain any sharp parts.

  7. Prototyping costs for the board game shall not exceed $200.

  8. The board pieces shall be recyclable.

  9. The tokens shall be sufficient for players to form the objective molecule, with a total quantity of at least 60 tokens.

  10. The token connectors shall be sufficient for players to piece the tokens together, with a total quantity of at least 100 connectors.

  11. A single round of the game takes at most 1 hour to complete, with 90% confidence.

  12. The board shall have at least 30 mission squares.
